Brief description of Peugeot 208

The new Peugeot 208 will impress you with its irresistible exterior design and technological innovations in the interior. These are just some of the reasons why this particular model was chosen as "Car of the Year" in Europe for 2020. The model is a typical city car, with LED automatic headlights with three distinctive features in the form of lion's claws and the latest version of the Peugeot i-Cockpit 3D dashboard (head-up display, compact steering wheel and touch screen). The high controls and the small steering wheel are sophisticated enough to be comfortable for drivers with different requirements and anatomical features.

Expert opinion by Car Rental

In addition to its appearance, Peugeot 208 will attract your attention with its dynamic and stable behavior on the road. A turbodiesel engine combined with a 6-speed manual transmission and 10" touch screen + navigation and DAB (Digital Audio Broadcasting) function are just some of the things that will contribute to an amazing road experience while driving. Extras such as cruise control, rear rear electronics and a 180-degree reversing camera make it the preferred choice for car rental.
